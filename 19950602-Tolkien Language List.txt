Esse Eru ontane meneli ar kemen.
Ar kemen ne kumna ar lusta,
ar mornier ne palmesse undumeo.
Ar palmesse i-linquion Erusuule nore.
Ar Eru quente: Ea kala; ar kala aine
Ar Eru kenne i maarie i kala; ar Eru uusange kala ho mornier.
Ar Eru i-kala estaneero arya, ar i-mornier estaneero loo.
Ar minya are e`ie anaroore ar anarnuuta.
 
Taare Eru quente: Ea endesse linquion palyale,
ar linqui ho linqui nai uusanga
Ar Eru estane palyale menel. 
Ar neuna are e`ie anaroore ar anarnuuta.
 
-------------------------------------------------------------
 
taare eru quente: sanga linqui undumeneleva ermenesse,
ar uutupa ea paraktale. Ar tana e`ie
Ar Eru paraktale entaneero kemen, 
ar entane i linqui sangaleva eareni.
Ar Eru kenne i maarie yana.
Ala tana Eru quente: tyar kemen laiqua salque,
erdetyaro salque; yaavetyaro alda kara i yaave ve nostalerwante,
i erdenta na entasse. Ar tana e`ie.
Tyarne kemen, taare, laiqua salque erdetyaro ve nostalerwante,
ar yaavetyaro alda, i erdenta na entasse ve nostalerwante.
Ar Eru kenne i maarie yana.
Ar nela are e`ie anaroore ar anarnuuta.
 
Ar quente Eru: ear menelkala palyalesse meneleva
uusangie loo arello; ar maare ea eeser luumis, aris, ar koranaris,
ar menelkalar palyalesse meneleva eante kalye amkemenesse.
Ar tana e`ie
Ar eru karane i atta altar menelkalar;
i alta menelkala heruie arya, 
ar i piinea menelkala heruie loo;
ar karaneero i eleni.
Ar Eru tulkeeronte palyalesse menelion ana kalyente amkemnesse,
ar heruie aryasse ar loosse ar an uusangie kala ho mornie.
Ar Eru kenne i maarie yana.
Ar kana are e`ie anaroore ar anarnuuta
 
_undumenel_ sky-under
_ermen_ one-place, some place.
_uutuupa_  V. un-cover
_paraktale_ < _parakta_+_le_ N.dry < _parak_+_ta_ V.dry < PE _parak_ A.dry
[compare _paraktie_ 'to dry, drying', N. 'drier']
_Ala tana_: ADV after that, beyond that, besides, also 
VK? [Q. _tarta_ < PE _thar-ta_ 'beyond that']
 
_erdetyaro_: A. seed-maker _erde_+_tyaro_ 
_nostale-rwa-nte_ kind-his-they > they of his kind
_erdenta_ fruit-they[gen.]
_entasse_ they [loc.sg]
_yaavetyaro_: A. fruit-maker _yaave_+_tyaro_
 
_nela_ 'third' < PE _nel_ 'three'
[as PE _mini_ 'one' > _mini-aa_ > Q. _minya_ 'first'] 
_menelkala_ N. sky-light
_eante_ be-they
_karane_ n-past < _kara_ V 'make' < PE {kar} > Q._karne_ 'built, made'
[maybe it should replace _ontane_ in _Eru ontane meneli ar kemen_]
_heruie_ 'ruling, to rule'
[_ana heru_ 'to rule' ?]
_kana_ 'fourth' < PE _kAnat_ 'four' > Q. _kanta_, _kan-_ four
